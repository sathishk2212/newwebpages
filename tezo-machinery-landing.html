OEOrder entry

Every manual order is a chance to build the wrong machine.

POs arrive by email, PDF, EDI and portal — then someone rekeys them into the ERP. Mismatched configs and fat-fingered options don't show up until production or shipping, where fixing them is expensive and visible to the customer.

The cost of rekeying
3–5% of order value lost to rework, returns and expedite freight.

How Tezo closes it

✓

Automated capture. OCR, EDI and portal intake read orders from any format and structure them automatically.

✓

Validation against the BOM. Every line checked against the configured machine before it ever hits the floor.

✓

Exception-only review. Clean orders flow straight through to ERP; humans touch only the ones that need judgment.
