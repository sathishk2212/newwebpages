How we solve it / the assessment

A two to four week assessment. To learn what's impacting your goals the most.

Your lens We run the engagement against your DORA metrics and AI-coding adoption timeline. The synthesis ranks initiatives by their effect on change failure rate first.

A two-hour working session with your QA, engineering, and product leads. We name the three questions this assessment has to answer.

→ Scope brief, signed

We map your test estate against business-critical paths — static analysis on the suite, plus the people who maintain it.

→ Coverage heat-map

Six months of production defects pulled, classified, and traced — then correlated to your AI-tooling adoption date, so cause and effect is visible, not assumed.

→ Leakage report

Candidate initiatives modeled for time, cost, and outcome — ranked by ROI. The ones we'd skip, named explicitly.

→ Initiative ledger

We walk the findings past your leads; anything that doesn't survive their pushback gets cut. Then a readout with the room you choose — deck, raw artifacts, recordings.
